— profile

Front-end fanatic, UX technician, Swiss Design enthusiast.

I am a designer at heart and code junkie by choice. Having no formal design or development education has been a journey of growth which has led to the culmination of nearly a decade’s worth of design and dev projects, large and small, successful and unsuccessful.

Finding balance between form and function and how they integrate into our daily lives through digital and analog channels has always been the foundation of my pursuit. Good design is hard. Great design is invisible. I believe that attention to craft in every detail, each pixel and line of code, serves to create a better product and experience.

What continues to motivate my conviction and skill couldn’t be better described than by this phrase imparted by the late Rajneesh Osho —

Creativity is the greatest rebellion in existence.
